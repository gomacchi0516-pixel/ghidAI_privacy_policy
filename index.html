Privacy Policy – GhidAI

Last updated: (add date)

1. Overview

GhidAI is an educational mobile application designed to help small and medium enterprises understand how to use AI and digital tools. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Contact Form

We collect your email address and message when you use the contact form. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 AI Assistant Interactions

Your text input is processed by our backend server and sent to OpenAI to generate responses. Messages may be temporarily stored in server logs for debugging and improvement. We do not collect personal information unless you include it in the messages.

2.3 No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party marketing tools.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To reply to your inquiries
  • To generate answers from the AI assistant
  • To maintain and improve the app’s functionality

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing. Messages are sent to OpenAI only for generating responses.

5. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption and limit access to server logs. However, no system can guarantee 100% security.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion or review of your stored data by contacting us.
