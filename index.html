Privacy Policy – GhidAI

Last updated: 05/12/2025

1. Overview

GhidAI is an educational mobile application designed to help small and medium enterprises understand how to use AI and digital tools. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Contact Form

When you use the contact form in the app, we collect your email address and your message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 AI Assistant Interactions

Your text input is processed by our backend server and then sent to OpenAI to generate a response. Messages may be temporarily stored in server logs for debugging and improvement. We do not collect personal information unless you include it in your messages.

2.3 No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use analytics tools, advertising trackers, or any third-party marketing services.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To reply to your inquiries
  • To generate responses from the AI assistant
  • To operate and improve the technical functionality of the app

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for marketing purposes.

Your messages are shared only with OpenAI for the purpose of generating AI responses.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical measures such as HTTPS encryption and limited access to server logs. However, no digital service can guarantee 100% security.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your contact form submissions or inquire about what data is stored. You may also request corrections of inaccurate data.

8. Contact

Email: contact@ghidai.ro

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Updated versions will be published on this same page.
